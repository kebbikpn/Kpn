Code of Conduct

Guidelines for maintaining respect, unity, and professionalism within the KPN community

Our Commitment

The Kebbi Progressive Network (KPN) is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for all members. This Code of Conduct outlines the standards of behavior expected from all KPN members, leaders, and participants.

Core Principles

Unity & Respect

Treat all members with dignity and respect, regardless of background, religion, ethnicity, or political affiliation.

Integrity & Honesty

Maintain honesty and transparency in all interactions and activities within the KPN community.

Professionalism

Conduct yourself professionally in all KPN activities, communications, and representations.

Accountability

Take responsibility for your actions and their impact on the KPN community and objectives.

Prohibited Behaviors

Zero Tolerance Policy

KPN has a zero-tolerance policy for the following behaviors:

Hate Speech
  • • Discriminatory language based on race, religion, gender, or ethnicity
  • • Inciting violence or hatred against individuals or groups
  • • Promoting divisive rhetoric within the KPN community
Fake News
  • • Spreading false or unverified information
  • • Sharing misleading content that could harm KPN's reputation
  • • Creating or promoting conspiracy theories
Indiscipline
  • • Disrupting KPN meetings or events
  • • Refusing to follow legitimate instructions from supervisors
  • • Engaging in behavior that undermines KPN objectives

Expected Standards of Behavior

  • Respectful Language: Use respectful and professional language in all communications
  • Constructive Feedback: Provide feedback that is helpful and aimed at improvement
  • Active Listening: Listen to others' perspectives and opinions with an open mind
  • Clear Communication: Express ideas clearly and avoid ambiguous statements
  • Timely Response: Respond to official communications within reasonable timeframes

  • Lead by Example: Demonstrate the values and behaviors expected from all members
  • Fair Decision Making: Make decisions that are fair, transparent, and in the best interest of KPN
  • Mentorship: Guide and support other members in their personal and professional growth
  • Accountability: Maintain accurate records of activities and report regularly to supervisors
  • Conflict Resolution: Address conflicts promptly and fairly

  • Active Engagement: Participate meaningfully in KPN activities and initiatives
  • Collaboration: Work cooperatively with other members towards common goals
  • Punctuality: Attend meetings and events on time
  • Preparation: Come prepared to meetings and events
  • Follow-through: Complete assigned tasks and commitments

  • Positive Representation: Represent KPN positively on all social media platforms
  • Accurate Information: Share only verified and accurate information
  • Engagement Requirements: Leaders must engage with official KPN Facebook page content
  • Professional Tone: Maintain a professional tone when commenting on KPN-related posts
  • Privacy Respect: Respect the privacy of other members and confidential KPN matters

Reporting Violations

How to Report

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

  1. Document the incident with details and evidence if available
  2. Report to your immediate supervisor or higher leadership
  3. Contact us through our official contact channels
  4. Message our Facebook page privately
Disciplinary Actions

Violations may result in:

  • Verbal Warning: For minor first-time offenses
  • Written Warning: For repeated or moderate violations
  • Suspension: Temporary removal from activities or positions
  • Removal: Permanent expulsion from KPN for severe violations

All disciplinary actions are documented and reviewable.

Our Collective Commitment

By joining KPN, every member commits to upholding these standards and contributing to a positive, respectful, and productive community environment.

"One Voice, One Change" - Together, we can build a better Kebbi State through unity, respect, and shared commitment to our values.
